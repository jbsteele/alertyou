AlertYou is an app soon to be available on the Salesforce AppExchange that provides you highly intelligent and predictive alerts on activities in your Salesforce.

Faster Sales Cycles

Alertyou will help you shorten sales cycles by keeping you on top of key tasks by providing you a list of key priorities everyday for you to follow up on.

Improved Customer Service

With better follow up on key accounts, leads, contacts, and opportunities, your customers and potential clients will choose to stay with you.

Updates Automatically

AlertYou will analyze your Salesforce data in real-time to keep you on top of the most important things going on in your Salesforce.

Key Features

Prioritization of Alerts

Alertyou’s advanced algorithm prioritizes your important to-do’s in your Salesforce automatically.

Real-time Sales Alerts

Alertyou will always be up to date with the latest alerts.

Customization of Alerts

For those who want to set up specific alerting for their Salesforce, we offer an interface to allow you to control alerting parameters.

Lightning and Classic Editions

Alertyou has been built to function in both editions giving you flexbility.

Emailed Alerts

Alertyou provides simple summarized reports on your key alerts and the health of your Salesforce.

Push Notifications

Alertyou integrates directly with Salesforce1 to give you alerts directly to your mobile device.
